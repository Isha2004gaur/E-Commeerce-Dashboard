# 🛒 Olist E-Commerce Performance Dashboard

##  Overview
Analysis of real e-commerce data from Olist — Brazil's largest online marketplace — to uncover insights around revenue, customer behavior, product performance and delivery efficiency.

---

##  Business Problem
- Which months drive the most revenue?
- Which product categories are most profitable?
- Who are our most valuable customers?
- How does delivery time affect customer satisfaction?

---

## 📂 Dataset
- **Source** : Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Dataset (Kaggle)
- **Size** : 100,000+ orders
- **Period** : 2017 - 2018
- **Tables** : 8 CSV files

---

---

##  Key Insights
- Total revenue of **20.03M BRL** generated in 2017-2018
- **November 2017** was best month driven by Black Friday
- **Bed Bath & Table** is #1 revenue category
- **São Paulo** has 3x more customers than any other state
- **71.7%** customers pay by credit card
- **35% customers are At Risk** — biggest win-back opportunity
- Average delivery time **12 days** with average review score **4.08/5**
